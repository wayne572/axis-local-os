# 01 - Product Overview

Status: active draft
Purpose: Explain AXIS in plain English.

## What AXIS Is

AXIS is a personal and business AI operating system.

It turns Claude from a general assistant into a structured operator that can help run work, clients, outreach, delivery, admin, priorities, relationships, and review cycles.

It is not a single prompt.

## Brand Structure

Wayne Francis is the public brand.

SF&W Project Solutions is the trading entity.

AXIS is the operating system / product logic developed by Wayne Francis and delivered through SF&W Project Solutions.

It is a folder-based operating system made of:
- startup instructions
- command triggers
- business context
- workflow files
- trackers
- templates
- compliance notes
- handover packs
- testing plans

## The Main Idea

Normal AI waits for a prompt.

AXIS gives the AI a role, a structure, and a job.

It tells the AI:
- who it serves
- what the current priorities are
- what mode to work in
- which files to read
- how to route work
- how to track progress
- how to review outputs
- what not to touch

## Why It Matters

Most people and small businesses do not need more AI noise.

They need:
- clearer priorities
- repeatable workflows
- better follow-up
- less admin drag
- faster delivery
- safer handover
- a system that remembers the work

AXIS is built to provide that structure.

## Current Strategic Priority

Wayne Francis is the brand. SF&W Project Solutions stays the trading entity and revenue engine.

The main commercial path is:
1. SME AI Automation Audit
2. Client OS build after audit
3. Client handover and 30-day review
4. Optional relationship connector and usage tracking
5. Solo Operator OS as a related offer lane

## What AXIS Is Not

AXIS is not:
- a generic chatbot
- a single SaaS product yet
- a replacement for professional legal, medical, financial, tax, or therapeutic advice
- a fully automated business without Wayne's review
- dependent on Hermes
- dependent on Black Map

## Current Host

The current working version is built for Claude.

Telegram and WhatsApp are planned access channels into the same Claude-led OS.

Hermes is archived.

Black Map is excluded unless Wayne explicitly brings it back.
