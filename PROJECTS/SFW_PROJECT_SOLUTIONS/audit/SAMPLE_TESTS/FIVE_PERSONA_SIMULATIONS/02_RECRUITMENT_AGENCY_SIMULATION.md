Candidate follow-up, client updates, and CRM hygiene.

Recruitment CRM, email, calendar, consultant notes.

## Audit Diagnosis

Key findings:
- Candidate and client updates are split across CRM, email, and personal notes.
- Follow-ups depend on consultant discipline.
- Repeated client updates are manually drafted.
- CRM hygiene affects team visibility.

Recommended first build:
Candidate Follow-Up And Client Update OS.

Why:
This is high-volume, repeatable, and directly affects revenue and client experience.
