## Demo Sequence

Five scenes, roughly 90 seconds each. Always follow the same beat:

```text
messy input -> Axis interpretation -> working asset -> next action
```

### Scene 1 - Voice Note To Action (0:45 - 2:15)

Show: notes app on screen. Paste in this fictional messy input.

```text
I need to call the accountant, sort that school form, reply to Sarah, figure out the website thing, and I keep forgetting the invoice. Also I had an idea for a short video about how we help landlords but I do not know where to put it.
```

Say:

```text
This is the kind of dump that lives in everyone's head at 9pm on a Sunday. Watch what Axis does with it.
```

Cut to the demo website Voice Note panel. Walk through what's on screen:

- summary
- tasks
- follow-ups
- admin risks
- a captured content idea
- next three actions
- a suggested tracker update

Key line:

```text
Notice it didn't just list things. It split tasks from follow-ups, parked the content idea where it won't get lost, and gave me three actions for the next twenty minutes. That's the difference between a chatbot and an operating system.
```

Boundary line (important):

```text
And when it shows a tracker update, it says "suggested" - because nothing changes in my files until I approve it.
```

### Scene 2 - Daily Command Centre (2:15 - 3:45)

Switch to the Daily Command Centre tab on the demo website.

Say:

```text
Once Axis has the raw input, it folds it into a daily plan.
```

Walk the screen left to right:

- top priorities for today
- open loops from yesterday
- follow-ups due
- one decision needed
- one thing to protect time for

Key line:

```text
This is what I open at 8am. It's not a to-do list. It's a daily operating plan that already knows what was sitting in my head last night.
```

### Scene 3 - Life Admin Tracker (3:45 - 5:15)

Switch tabs. Type or paste this messy input live:

```text
I have bills, appointments, forms, renewals, and family stuff everywhere. I do not know what matters first.
```

Cut to the Life Admin Tracker view. Show the priority split:

- Today
- This week
- Book next

Reference the council tax / form / dentist example from `OUTCOME_EXAMPLES.md` if helpful.

Key line:

```text
Most people don't have a life admin problem. They have a "what matters first" problem. Axis answers that in one screen.
```

Before / after moment - say it out loud:

```text
Before: fifteen open loops in my head, none of them moving.
After: three I'll do today, four parked with a date, and a tracker that remembers them so I don't have to.
```

### Scene 4 - Small Business Follow-Up (5:15 - 7:00)

Switch to the Lead Tracker tab. Paste:

```text
I am getting leads from WhatsApp, Instagram, referrals, and email. I forget to follow up and I do not have a proper quote process.
```

Walk through what appears:

- lead tracker with context, next step, follow-up date, state
- a draft follow-up message
- a weekly revenue action
- a simple sales dashboard

Read the draft follow-up message out loud (the one from `OUTCOME_EXAMPLES.md`). Then say:

```text
Every small business owner I work with loses money in this exact gap - between "interesting conversation" and "I'll follow up tomorrow". Axis closes that gap.
```

### Scene 5 - Website From A Sentence (7:00 - 8:30)

Switch to the Website Build preview tab. Paste:

```text
I need a simple website for my mobile hair business. I want people to see what I do, prices, reviews, and book a consultation.
```

Show:

- the page structure (hero, services, trust, reviews, booking, FAQ)
- the actual HTML preview rendering in the browser
- the launch checklist

Key line:

```text
One sentence in. A working first version out. Not a finished site - but a real starting point you can hand to a designer, or launch as-is in an afternoon.
```

Here's the honest before-and-after.
Before: my work and life admin lived in seven places - voice notes, WhatsApp, notebooks, my inbox, my head. Things slipped.
