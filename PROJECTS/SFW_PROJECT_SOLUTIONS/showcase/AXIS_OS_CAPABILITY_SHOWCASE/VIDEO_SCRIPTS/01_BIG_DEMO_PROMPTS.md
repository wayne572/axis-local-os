TRIGGER:

```text
AXIS: VOICE NOTE TO ACTION
```

```text
I need to call the accountant, sort that school form, reply to Sarah, figure out the website thing, and I keep forgetting the invoice. Also I had an idea for a short video about how we help landlords but I do not know where to put it.
```

EXPECTED OUTPUT shape:

- Summary: one sentence

- Follow-ups: 1 item (reply to Sarah)
- Open question: 1 item (website thing - needs clarification)
- Captured idea: 1 item (landlord video) parked under content
- Admin risk: forgotten invoice = revenue risk
- Next three actions, in order
- Suggested tracker update: add invoice + school form to admin tracker

KEY LINE TO SAY ON CAMERA:

```text
Notice it didn't just list things. It split tasks from follow-ups, parked the content idea where it won't get lost, and gave me three actions for the next twenty minutes.
```
