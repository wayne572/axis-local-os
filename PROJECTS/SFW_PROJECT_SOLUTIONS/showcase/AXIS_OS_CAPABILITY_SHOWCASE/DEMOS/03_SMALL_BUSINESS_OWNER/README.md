# Demo 03 - Small Business Owner

Status: active draft

## Demo Input

```text
I am getting leads from WhatsApp, Instagram, referrals, and email. I forget to follow up and I do not have a proper quote process.
```

## Axis Output

### Problem

Leads are coming in, but there is no single follow-up system.

### Build

- lead tracker
- quote request checklist
- follow-up message template
- weekly revenue review

### Suggested Lead Tracker

| Lead | Source | Need | Next Step | Follow-Up Date | State |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Demo lead 1 | WhatsApp | Quote | Ask for details | Today | active |
| Demo lead 2 | Instagram | Service info | Send package summary | Tomorrow | active |
| Demo lead 3 | Referral | Discovery | Offer short call | Friday | waiting |

### Follow-Up Draft

```text
Hi [Name], just following up on your enquiry. To give you the right price, can you send me a quick note on what you need, your preferred date, and any deadline you are working to?
```

## Demo Value

Shows direct money value: fewer missed leads and better follow-up.
