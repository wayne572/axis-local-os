# Demo 01 - Voice Note To Action

Status: active draft

## Demo Input

```text
I need to call the accountant, sort that school form, reply to Sarah, figure out the website thing, and I keep forgetting the invoice. Also I had an idea for a short video about how we help landlords but I do not know where to put it.
```

### Summary

You have five open loops:

- accountant call
- school form
- Sarah reply
- website decision
- unpaid or unsent invoice

You also have one content idea:

- short landlord-focused video

1. Send or check the invoice.
2. Complete the school form.
3. Reply to Sarah with a short update.
