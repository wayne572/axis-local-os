# Demo Scenarios

Status: active draft
Purpose: Menu of demonstration scenarios for prospects, testers, and YouTube videos.

## 1. Voice Note To Action

Audience:
Anyone who feels mentally overloaded.

Messy input:

```text
I need to call the accountant, sort that school form, reply to Sarah, figure out the website thing, and I keep forgetting the invoice. Also I had an idea for a short video about how we help landlords but I do not know where to put it.
```

Axis output:

- summary
- tasks
- follow-ups
- admin risks
- content idea
- next three actions
- suggested tracker update

## 2. Life Admin Rescue

Audience:
Parents, carers, busy professionals, solo operators.

Messy input:

```text
I have bills, appointments, forms, renewals, and family stuff everywhere. I do not know what matters first.
```

Axis output:

- urgent / soon / can wait
- three first actions
- admin tracker
- reminder plan
- message draft if needed

## 3. Small Business Owner

Audience:
Trades, beauty, cleaning, coaching, property, consulting, service businesses.

Messy input:

```text
I am getting leads from WhatsApp, Instagram, referrals, and email. I forget to follow up and I do not have a proper quote process.
```

Axis output:

- lead tracker
- quote follow-up script
- client onboarding checklist
- weekly revenue action
- simple sales dashboard

## 4. Content Creator

- video outline
- repurpose plan

- follow-up email

Audience:
Business owners considering an audit.

## 9. Airtable Database Demo

Audience:
SME owners, operators, and prospects who want one place to store leads, audit answers, build scope, delivery tasks, and reviews.

Messy input:

```text
I already have an Airtable base, but I do not want it touched. I need to show how AXIS could create a safe new table/database structure for client delivery, using a fictional SME as an example.
```

Axis output:

- safety boundary around existing Airtable base
- new demo base/table plan
- demo client profile
- audit capture table
- opportunity map table
- delivery task table
- sample rows
- form and view recommendations
