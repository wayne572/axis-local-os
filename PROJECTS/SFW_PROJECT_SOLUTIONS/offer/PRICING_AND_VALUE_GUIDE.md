## Objection Responses

### "Why not just use Notion or Airtable?"

Those are tools. AXIS is the operating structure around how the business works. The value is not the page or database. It is the workflow, command structure, review cycle, handover logic, and decision discipline.

### "Why not build it ourselves?"

You can. Most teams lose 60-100 hours trying to design the structure, connect the workflows, and decide what matters. AXIS starts from a tested operating model and adapts it to the business.

### "Why does the audit cost what it does?"

The audit prevents expensive wrong moves. It identifies where automation will actually save time, improve follow-up, or protect delivery before the client spends money building the wrong thing. The ladder lets you start at GBP 2,500 (Lite) for a focused look or step up to GBP 6,500 (Deep Dive) for full operational coverage.

### "Why does the build cost what it does?"

The build replaces months of internal trial and error. It creates the operating system, documents the workflow, trains the client, and hands over a system they own. Tiered from GBP 6,500 (Foundation) to GBP 12,500 (Enterprise) by scope and complexity.

### "Why pay for OS Care Plan after the build?"

Systems drift as teams grow. OS Care Plan keeps the system current, reviews new bottlenecks, improves workflows, and gives expert operations guidance without hiring a full-time operations person. No lock-in. From GBP 750 per month.

### "Can we just do the bundle and skip the separate audit?"

Yes. Audit + Build Bundle is for clients who already know they want the implementation and want the combined diagnosis-plus-build at a lower total cost. Scale tier (GBP 12,995) is the recommended default.
