## Objection Responses

### "Why not just use Notion or Airtable?"

Those are tools. AXIS is the operating structure around how the business works. The value is not the page or database. It is the workflow, command structure, review cycle, handover logic, and decision discipline.

### "Why not build it ourselves?"

You can. Most teams lose 60-100 hours trying to design the structure, connect the workflows, and decide what matters. AXIS starts from a tested operating model and adapts it to the business.

### "Why does the audit cost GBP 5,595?"

The audit prevents expensive wrong moves. It identifies where automation will actually save time, improve follow-up, or protect delivery before the client spends money building the wrong thing.

### "Why does the build cost GBP 9,995?"

The build replaces months of internal trial and error. It creates the operating system, documents the workflow, trains the client, and hands over a system they own.

### "Why pay for Connector Mode after the build?"

Systems drift as teams grow. Connector Mode keeps the system current, reviews new bottlenecks, improves workflows, and gives expert operations guidance without hiring a full-time operations person.
