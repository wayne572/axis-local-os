## Decision

Consultancy comes first.

The first repeatable offer should be a simple AI Automation Audit for UK SMEs. The audit should lead with operational pain, not AI novelty. Its job is to start low-pressure conversations, identify automation opportunities, and create a path into paid implementation.

SF&W should not limit itself to one sector. The core offer should stay broad enough for SMEs across different industries, while the outreach and examples can be adapted by sector.

## Target Market

UK SMEs that are losing time to repetitive admin, slow follow-up, scattered data, manual reporting, or disconnected tools.

Priority sectors:
- Estate agents and property businesses
- Recruitment agencies
- Accountancy firms
- Property management companies
- Care homes and care providers
- Cleaning, facilities, trades, and local service businesses
- Small consultancies and professional services firms

Common pain points:
- Enquiries arrive across too many channels.
- Staff copy and paste the same information repeatedly.
- Follow-ups depend on memory instead of process.
- Customer, client, tenant, candidate, or patient updates are handled manually.
- Documents, emails, spreadsheets, CRMs, and calendars do not join up.
- Managers cannot easily see workload, status, or next actions.

## Funnel

### Step 1 - Lead Magnet

Working title:
Where SMEs Lose Hours Every Week To Admin

Format:
One-page checklist or short PDF.

Call to action:
Book a free AI Automation Audit.

### Step 2 - Audit Landing Page

Core message:
Practical AI automation for SMEs that want faster follow-up, cleaner admin, and less manual work.

Page sections:
- Problem: enquiries, follow-ups, reporting, client updates, admin handoffs, repeated manual tasks.
- Offer: free AI Automation Audit.
- What Wayne reviews: workflows, tools, handoffs, response delays, manual repeat tasks.
- Output: automation map and priority plan.
- Trust: plain-English, no hype, no platform lock-in.
- CTA: book a free audit call.

### Step 3 - Outreach

Target:
UK SME owners, directors, and operations leads.

Outreach angle:
Operational improvement, not AI transformation.

Message type:
Short LinkedIn or email message offering a free workflow audit.

### Step 4 - Discovery Call

Call objective:
Find one painful, repeated workflow that can be automated or improved.

Questions:
- Where do enquiries first arrive?
- What gets copied or re-keyed manually?
- Which follow-ups are often late?
- Which admin task happens every day but should not need a person each time?
- What tools are already in use?
- What would save the team the most time in the next 30 days?

### Step 5 - Paid Implementation Path

Possible starter projects:
- Enquiry triage and response drafts
- Follow-up automation
- New client or customer intake
- Document and email processing
- Internal admin tracker
- Weekly reporting dashboard
- CRM or spreadsheet clean-up workflow
- Staff knowledge base or FAQ assistant
