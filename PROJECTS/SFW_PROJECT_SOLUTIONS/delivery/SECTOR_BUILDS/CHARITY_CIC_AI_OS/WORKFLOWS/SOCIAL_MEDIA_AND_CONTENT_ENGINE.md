# Social Media And Content Engine

Status: active
Purpose: Specialist social media, image-prompt, caption, comment, and campaign content layer for charity/CIC AI OS builds.

## Purpose

Many charities and CICs rely heavily on social media to build trust, raise funds, recruit volunteers, explain their work, and keep supporters emotionally connected.

This engine helps the organisation create consistent, safe, mission-led content without needing a full marketing team.

## Core Jobs

The OS helps with:
- post ideas
- image generation prompts
- Canva/design briefs
- captions
- comment replies
- donor thank-you posts
- volunteer recruitment posts
- fundraising campaign content
- impact story posts
- awareness posts
- event promotion
- partner shout-outs
- weekly content planning
- content repurposing across platforms

## Platform Notes

Use platform-specific formats where relevant:
- Instagram: image-led posts, reels captions, carousel ideas, story prompts
- Facebook: community updates, event posts, fundraising appeals, longer captions
- LinkedIn: partner updates, funder visibility, governance/impact credibility
- TikTok/Reels/Shorts: short scripts, hooks, captions, visual shot lists
- X/Twitter: short updates, threads, campaign reminders
- WhatsApp broadcast/community: short warm updates, event reminders, volunteer asks

## Content Pillars

Recommended charity/CIC pillars:
- who we help
- why the work matters
- what happened this week
- volunteer stories
- donor and sponsor thanks
- funding need
- impact evidence
- behind the scenes
- upcoming events
- partner collaboration
- myth-busting and awareness
- urgent calls to action

## Image Prompt Rules

When creating prompts for images:
- describe the intended platform and format
- include the emotion and message
- avoid exploiting vulnerable people
- avoid showing identifiable beneficiaries unless permission is confirmed
- use anonymised, symbolic, or scene-based visuals where appropriate
- mention brand colours, logo placement, and text area if known
- avoid overcrowded text in images
- create alt text for accessibility

## Comment And Reply Rules

The OS can draft:
- thank-you replies
- supporter replies
- volunteer enquiry replies
- donor acknowledgement replies
- signposting replies
- escalation responses for complaints or sensitive comments

Human review is required for:
- complaints
- safeguarding references
- personal stories
- media enquiries
- hostile comments
- legal or policy issues
- requests for direct support

## Prompt Pack

### Weekly Content Plan

```text
Create a 7-day social media plan for our charity/CIC using these priorities:
[paste priorities]

Include post topic, platform, caption angle, image idea, call to action, and any human-review risks.
```

### Image Prompt

```text
Create 5 image-generation prompts for this charity/CIC post:
[describe post]

Make them warm, respectful, non-exploitative, and suitable for [Instagram/Facebook/LinkedIn/etc.].
Include alt text for each image.
```

### Canva Brief

```text
Turn this post idea into a Canva design brief:
[paste idea]

Include format, headline, image direction, colours, layout, accessibility notes, and caption.
```

### Caption Builder

```text
Write 3 caption options for this post:
[paste context]

Create one warm, one direct, and one fundraising-focused version. Keep claims evidence-based.
```

### Impact Story Post

```text
Turn these notes into an anonymised impact story for social media:
[paste notes]

Do not include names, identifying details, unsupported outcomes, or anything that needs safeguarding review.
Add a human-review checklist.
```

### Fundraising Appeal Post

```text
Create a fundraising appeal post for this need:
[paste need]

Include a short caption, longer caption, image prompt, suggested hashtags, and a clear call to action.
Do not use pressure tactics or unsupported claims.
```

### Comment Replies

```text
Draft replies to these social media comments:
[paste comments]

Group them as thank-you, question, volunteer interest, donor interest, complaint, or sensitive. Flag any that need human review.
```

### Reel Or Short Video Script

```text
Create 5 short-form video ideas for this charity/CIC:
[paste goal]

Include hook, 15-30 second script, shot list, caption, image/thumbnail prompt, and call to action.
```

## Integrity Rules

The OS must not:
- identify beneficiaries without permission
- use poverty, trauma, or crisis in an exploitative way
- fabricate quotes, outcomes, or statistics
- imply guaranteed results
- respond to safeguarding or support requests without human review
- publish public statements without approval

The OS should always flag:
- missing consent
- missing evidence
- sensitive content
- comments needing escalation
- claims that need proof
- images that could identify someone
