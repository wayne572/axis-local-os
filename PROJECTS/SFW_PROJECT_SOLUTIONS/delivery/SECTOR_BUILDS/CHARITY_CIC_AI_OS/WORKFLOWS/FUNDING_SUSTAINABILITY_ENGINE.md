# Funding Sustainability Engine

Status: active
Purpose: Specialist grant, fundraising, donor, and sustainability workflow layer for charity/CIC AI OS builds.

## Purpose

This engine helps the organisation continue the work it is doing by making funding work visible, repeatable, and evidence-led.

It supports:
- finding and tracking suitable grants
- preparing stronger applications
- managing donor and sponsor relationships
- planning fundraising campaigns
- capturing ethical impact evidence
- meeting funder reporting requirements
- identifying income risk before it becomes a crisis

## Specialist Workflows

### Grant Readiness

Checks whether the organisation has:
- clear mission summary
- project description
- need statement
- evidence of demand
- target beneficiaries
- outcomes and outputs
- budget
- policies and governance documents
- accounts or financial summary
- impact evidence
- delivery plan

Prompt:

```text
Run a grant readiness check and show what is missing before we apply.
```

### Funder Fit Check

Checks:
- eligibility
- geography
- beneficiary group
- funding priorities
- grant size
- deadline
- restrictions
- reporting burden
- match funding
- likelihood of fit

Prompt:

```text
Check this funder against our organisation and tell me whether it is worth pursuing.
```

### Grant Application Builder

Supports:
- question breakdown
- answer outline
- first draft
- evidence checklist
- budget narrative
- human review checklist

Prompt:

```text
Turn this grant form into a draft application plan and mark every claim that needs evidence.
```

### Donor And Sponsor Stewardship

Supports:
- thank-you messages
- update schedules
- renewal asks
- sponsor benefit tracking
- relationship notes
- warm follow-ups

Prompt:

```text
Create a stewardship plan for these donors and draft the next thank-you or update message.
```

### Fundraising Campaign Builder

Supports:
- campaign goal
- target audience
- story angle
- message sequence
- launch checklist
- partner asks
- progress updates
- thank-you plan

Prompt:

```text
Build a fundraising campaign plan for this project with messages, dates, and follow-up actions.
```

### Impact Evidence Capture

Supports:
- outputs
- outcomes
- anonymised stories
- quote permissions
- evidence gaps
- funder-ready summaries

Prompt:

```text
Review these delivery notes and turn them into anonymised impact evidence for a funder update.
```

### Funder Reporting And Renewal

Supports:
- report deadlines
- required evidence
- spend summary prompts
- outcome summaries
- renewal opportunity flags
- next application planning

Prompt:

```text
Prepare a funder reporting checklist and flag what we need before the deadline.
```

### Income Risk Review

Supports:
- funding gaps
- concentration risk
- grant end dates
- unreported grants
- missed follow-ups
- next 30/60/90 day actions

Prompt:

```text
Run an income risk review and show the next funding actions for the next 90 days.
```

## Integrity Rules

The OS must:
- use only evidence the organisation provides
- flag missing evidence
- keep beneficiary stories anonymised unless permission is recorded
- mark grant applications and public fundraising copy `in review`
- ask for human sign-off before submission or publication

The OS must not:
- invent outcomes
- inflate numbers
- fabricate testimonials
- imply guaranteed funding
- submit applications
- provide regulated financial or legal advice
