# Charity And CIC Deployment Modules

Status: active
Purpose: Generic workflow modules available for charity/CIC AI OS delivery.

## Visibility Rule

These modules are available to the AI OS, but they should not be presented as a long list to the organisation during onboarding.

Use them behind the scenes to support natural conversation, advice, drafting, and next-step selection.

Charity-facing language should talk about outcomes, not modules.

## Module 1 - Command Centre

Purpose:
Create a simple operating view of priorities, overdue actions, decisions, blockers, and sensitive items requiring human review.

Outputs:
- daily action list
- weekly priority view
- blocker list
- decision prompts

## Module 2 - Referral And Enquiry Tracker

Purpose:
Capture, acknowledge, and follow up new referrals or enquiries without storing unnecessary sensitive detail.

Outputs:
- referral/enquiry record
- follow-up reminder
- draft acknowledgement
- human-review flag where needed

## Module 3 - Grants And Funding Pipeline

Purpose:
Track grant opportunities, funder fit, eligibility, requirements, deadlines, applications, reports, and renewals.

Outputs:
- grant pipeline
- deadline view
- fit check
- evidence checklist
- application task list
- reporting and renewal reminders

## Module 4 - Grant Application Support

Purpose:
Help the organisation prepare stronger grant applications using only real evidence and human-reviewed claims.

Outputs:
- application question breakdown
- draft answers
- project need statement
- budget narrative prompts
- evidence gap list
- final review checklist

## Module 5 - Fundraising Campaign Tracker

Purpose:
Plan and track appeals, community fundraising, events, sponsor asks, and supporter journeys.

Outputs:
- campaign plan
- message sequence
- launch checklist
- follow-up list
- campaign progress summary

## Module 6 - Donor And Sponsor Stewardship

Purpose:
Keep donor, sponsor, and supporter relationships warm, thanked, updated, and ready for renewal.

Outputs:
- thank-you drafts
- supporter update plan
- sponsor follow-up notes
- renewal reminders
- relationship history

## Module 7 - Impact Evidence And Funder Reporting

Purpose:
Capture outputs, outcomes, anonymised stories, reporting requirements, and evidence gaps.

Outputs:
- funder update draft
- impact summary
- evidence checklist
- anonymised story draft
- report deadline tracker

## Module 8 - Income Risk And Continuation Review

Purpose:
Identify funding gaps, grant end dates, funder dependency, and the next sustainability actions.

Outputs:
- income risk summary
- next 30/60/90 day funding actions
- renewal opportunity list
- board funding update

## Module 9 - Social Media And Content Engine

Purpose:
Create practical social media content support for image prompts, captions, comments, campaigns, and platform-specific posting.

Outputs:
- weekly content plan
- image-generation prompts
- Canva/design briefs
- caption options
- comment reply drafts
- fundraising campaign post pack
- volunteer recruitment post pack
- short video/reel ideas
- human-review flags

## Module 10 - Volunteer And Partner Memory

Purpose:
Keep relationship notes, follow-up commitments, meeting context, and warm introductions visible.

Outputs:
- relationship memory
- partner brief
- follow-up reminders
- meeting prep notes

## Module 11 - Service Delivery Tracker

Purpose:
Track programme activity, sessions, operational blockers, and non-sensitive delivery updates.

Outputs:
- service delivery tracker
- overdue action list
- blocker escalation note
- weekly status summary

## Module 12 - Governance And Board Reporting

Purpose:
Prepare clear summaries for trustees, directors, boards, funders, or leadership meetings.

Outputs:
- board-ready update
- decision log
- risk notes
- monthly progress summary

## Build Order

Recommended:

1. Command Centre
2. Grants And Funding Pipeline
3. Grant Readiness And Evidence Checklist
4. Referral And Enquiry Tracker
5. Communications Drafting
6. Fundraising Campaign Tracker
7. Donor And Sponsor Stewardship
8. Impact Evidence And Funder Reporting
9. Social Media And Content Engine
10. Volunteer And Partner Memory
11. Governance And Board Reporting

## Scope Rule

Only activate modules included in the agreed organisation scope.

Sensitive modules stay `in review` until data and safeguarding boundaries are confirmed.

Funding modules stay `in review` until claims, numbers, stories, budgets, and submission responsibilities are confirmed.

Social media modules stay `in review` until platforms, brand rules, consent rules, and post approval responsibilities are confirmed.

## Hidden Success Tools

The following support tools are internal helpers and should not appear as onboarding options:
- first win selector
- impact evidence vault
- make-this-safer review
- monthly funding rhythm
- board/trustee pack builder
- volunteer activation kit
- local partnership builder
- overwhelmed week mode
- before/after success log
- advice offer filter
