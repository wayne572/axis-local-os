# Charity And CIC Data Handling Notes

Status: in review
Purpose: Practical data handling guidance for a charity/CIC AI OS.

## Rule

Only collect and store what the agreed workflow needs.

Use anonymised or minimised information wherever possible.

## Personal Data

Personal data may include:
- names
- emails
- phone numbers
- roles
- volunteer notes
- donor notes
- referral notes linked to a person

Use only for the agreed workflow.

## Sensitive Data

Do not store special category or safeguarding-related data unless explicitly agreed and reviewed.

Sensitive data may include:
- health
- religion
- political opinions
- ethnicity
- biometric data
- union membership
- details about children or vulnerable adults
- safeguarding disclosures
- immigration status
- financial hardship
- criminal records information

## Organisation Responsibilities

The organisation remains responsible for:
- lawful basis
- privacy notices
- internal access control
- data accuracy
- deletion requests
- safeguarding procedures
- final decisions about support, access, risk, or escalation

## SF&W Responsibilities

SF&W should:
- build only agreed workflows
- flag compliance and safeguarding risks
- avoid unnecessary data collection
- support deletion or correction requests where relevant
- keep the first version narrow until the workflow is proven

## Review

Mark compliance as `in review` if:
- personal data is unclear
- sensitive data appears
- safeguarding is involved
- a new tool is added
- data leaves the organisation's usual systems
- usage tracking or analytics are enabled
- public impact stories or testimonials are being drafted
- donor, beneficiary, volunteer, or partner data is being reused in a new way

## Safeguarding Boundary

The AI OS may help organise information, prepare summaries, and draft routine communications.

The AI OS must not make safeguarding decisions, eligibility decisions, clinical decisions, legal decisions, financial decisions, or final decisions about access to support.

Sensitive outputs require human review.

## Usage Tracking

Usage tracking must be transparent and limited.

Review:

- `COMPLIANCE/USAGE_TRACKING_NOTICE.md`
- `COMPLIANCE/CLIENT_CONSENT_AND_ANALYTICS.md`
- `BUSINESS/TRACKING/USAGE_LOG.md`
