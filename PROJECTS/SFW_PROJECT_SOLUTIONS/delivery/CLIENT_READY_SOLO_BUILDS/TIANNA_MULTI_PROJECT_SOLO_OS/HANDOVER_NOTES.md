Tianna should leave the handover knowing:

- how to park ideas without losing them

6. Complete the first section of `SETUP_QUESTIONNAIRE.md`.
7. Add at least three active projects.
8. Add at least three open loops.
9. Add one life admin task.
10. Add one pending decision.
11. Run a mini weekly review.

- Tianna knows what to do first each day.
