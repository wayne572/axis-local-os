# Welcome Savannah

Hi Savannah,

I am your new Solo Operator OS for SCC Homes.

I am here to help you keep the moving parts of your service accommodation business clearer, calmer, and easier to act on.

That means helping you with the things that often spread across your head, phone, inbox, notes, and WhatsApp:

- guest enquiries
- check-in and check-out questions
- booking admin
- cleaner and changeover follow-ups
- supplier and maintenance tasks
- property standards
- guest experience
- reviews
- business ideas
- weekly priorities
- decisions and open loops

You do not need to use technical language.

You can type a messy note, a voice-note transcript, a guest message, a property issue, or a business idea, and I will help turn it into structure.

## Start With This

Open this folder in Codex and type:

```text
AXIS: SOLO START
```

The first time you use the system, I will ask a few setup questions. One of them is whether you would like to give your AI PA a name, or whether you want to keep the default name Axis.

After setup, you can use the system daily for:

- what matters today
- who needs a reply
- which booking or property task is urgent
- what needs chasing
- what can wait
- what should be reviewed before it becomes a problem

The goal is not to make SCC Homes more complicated.

The goal is to help you see what matters, act faster, and stop important things from slipping.
