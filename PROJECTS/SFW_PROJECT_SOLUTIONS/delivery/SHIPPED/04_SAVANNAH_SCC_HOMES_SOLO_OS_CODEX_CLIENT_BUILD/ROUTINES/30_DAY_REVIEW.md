# 30 Day Review

Status: active
Purpose: Review whether the Solo Operator OS is improving Savannah's clarity, SCC Homes operations, guest experience, and follow-through.

## Trigger

```text
AXIS: 30 DAY REVIEW
```

## Review Areas

### 1. Clarity

Questions:
- Is the user clearer on what matters each day?
- Are priorities easier to choose?
- Is the OS reducing mental clutter?

### 2. Follow-Through

Questions:
- Are fewer tasks being dropped?
- Are follow-ups happening sooner?
- Are open loops easier to see?

### 3. Personal Admin

Questions:
- Which admin items were cleared?
- Which admin items are still stuck?
- What needs a stronger reminder or process?

### 4. Business Or Work Progress

Questions:
- What booking, revenue, guest experience, visibility, or property actions moved forward?
- What is still too vague?
- What should become a repeatable workflow?

### 4A. SCC Homes Operations

Questions:
- Are guest enquiries easier to manage?
- Are cleaner, supplier, and maintenance follow-ups clearer?
- Are property tasks being closed faster?
- Are guest issues being handled calmly and consistently?
- Are review or reputation actions being captured?

### 5. Relationships

Questions:
- Were useful follow-ups made?
- Were any introductions or opportunities created?
- Is relationship tracking respectful and useful?

### 6. System Fit

Questions:
- Which commands are being used?
- Which commands are ignored?
- What needs simplifying?
- What should be added?
