# Solo Operator OS Setup Questionnaire

Status: active draft
Purpose: Capture the minimum context needed to configure the OS.

## User Identity

1. What should the OS call you?
2. Would you like to give your AI PA a name, or should we keep the default name Axis?
3. What timezone are you in?
4. You are starting in Claude. Do you expect to use Telegram, WhatsApp, or another route later?

## Life And Work Areas

1. What are the main areas this OS should support?
2. What do you currently manage on your own?
3. What usually gets forgotten, delayed, or avoided?
4. What is taking up too much mental space?

## Business Or Work

1. Do you run a business, side project, freelance work, or career portfolio?
2. What are the main revenue or progress goals?
3. What offers, services, products, or projects matter most right now?
4. What follow-ups or opportunities are already open?

## Personal Admin

1. What admin categories need tracking?
2. Are there any urgent renewals, forms, bills, bookings, or appointments?
3. Where do important documents currently live?
4. What admin would create risk if ignored?

## Relationships

1. Who are the important people to stay in touch with?
2. Are there any introductions, referrals, or follow-ups already pending?
3. Are there any boundaries around what should not be tracked?

## Weekly Operating Rhythm

1. What does a good week look like?
2. When should the weekly review happen?
3. What does the user want more of?
4. What does the user want less of?

## Setup Output

After setup, update:
- `PERSONAL_CONTEXT.md`
- `TRACKERS/ACTIVE_LOOPS.md`
- `LIFE_ADMIN/PERSONAL_ADMIN_TRACKER.md`
- `BUSINESS/SOLO_BUSINESS_TRACKER.md`
- `RELATIONSHIPS/RELATIONSHIP_MEMORY.md`

Mark setup as `in review` until the user confirms the context is accurate.
