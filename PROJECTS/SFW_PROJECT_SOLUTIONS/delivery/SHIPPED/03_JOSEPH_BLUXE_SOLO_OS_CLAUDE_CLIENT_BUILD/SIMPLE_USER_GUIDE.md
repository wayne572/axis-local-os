# Simple User Guide

Prepared for: Joseph / Bluxe

## What This Is

This is your personal AI operating system.

It is designed to help you think, plan, build, research, draft, organise, and follow through.

It is not just a place to ask questions.

It is a place to get work moving.

You can use it for Bluxe, personal admin, content ideas, business ideas, follow-ups, decisions, research, simple systems, and weekly planning.

You do not need to use technical language. Just tell it what is going on, and it will help you turn that into something useful.

## What It Can Help You Do

You can use this OS to:

- plan your day
- shape your week
- build business ideas
- research opportunities
- draft website copy
- draft emails and messages
- create content ideas
- prepare offers
- organise admin
- track follow-ups
- capture relationship notes
- think through decisions
- create simple workflows
- turn messy notes into actions
- review what is working
- keep momentum on Bluxe

You can start rough.

The OS will help create structure.

## How To Start

Open this folder in Claude.

Then type:

```text
AXIS: SOLO START
```

The OS will ask a few setup questions.

It will also ask whether you want to give it a name, or keep the default name Axis.

## The Main Commands

### 1. Daily Priorities

Use this when you want to work out what matters today.

```text
AXIS: DAILY COMMAND
```

Good for:

- choosing today's top priorities
- spotting what is overdue
- deciding what can wait
- getting one clear next action
- cutting through noise

Example:

```text
AXIS: DAILY COMMAND

I have too many things going on today. Help me decide what matters first.
```

### 2. Capture A Thought

Use this when you have a messy note, idea, message, or reminder.

```text
AXIS: CAPTURE THIS
```

Good for:

- ideas
- follow-ups
- rough notes
- voice note transcripts
- content thoughts
- business ideas
- tasks you do not want to forget

Example:

```text
AXIS: CAPTURE THIS

I have an idea for Bluxe but it is rough. Help me turn it into a simple plan.
```

### 3. Business Builder

Use this when you want to move Bluxe forward.


- research

- growth tasks


Good for:

## What Not To Use It For

Do not use it as a replacement for:

- legal advice
- tax advice
- financial advice
- medical advice
- therapy
- safeguarding support
- regulated professional advice

It can help you organise your thinking, but you remain responsible for decisions.

## Best Way To Get Value

Use it little and often.

Do not wait until your notes are perfect.

The best thing to say is:

```text
Here is the mess. Help me sort the next action.
```

Or:

```text
Here is the idea. Help me build the first version.
```
