# Data Handling Notes

Status: in review
Purpose: Practical data handling guidance for the client OS.

## Rule

Only collect and store what the workflow needs.

## Personal Data

Personal data may include:
- names
- emails
- phone numbers
- job titles
- business notes linked to a person

Use only for the agreed workflow.

## Sensitive Data

Do not store special category data unless explicitly agreed and reviewed.

Special category data may include:
- health
- religion
- political opinions
- ethnicity
- biometric data
- union membership

## Client Responsibilities

The client remains responsible for:
- lawful basis
- privacy notices
- internal access control
- data accuracy
- deletion requests

## SF&W Responsibilities

SF&W should:
- build only agreed workflows
- flag compliance risks
- avoid unnecessary data collection
- support deletion or correction requests where relevant

## Review

Mark compliance as `in review` if:
- outreach is involved
- personal data is unclear
- sensitive data appears
- a new tool is added
- data leaves the client's usual systems
- usage tracking or analytics are enabled

## Usage Tracking

Usage tracking must be transparent and limited.

- `COMPLIANCE/USAGE_TRACKING_NOTICE.md`
- `COMPLIANCE/CLIENT_CONSENT_AND_ANALYTICS.md`
