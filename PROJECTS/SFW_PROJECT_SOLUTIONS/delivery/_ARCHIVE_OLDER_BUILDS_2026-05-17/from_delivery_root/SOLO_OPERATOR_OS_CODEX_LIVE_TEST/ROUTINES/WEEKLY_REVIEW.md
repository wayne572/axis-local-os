# Weekly Review

Status: active
Purpose: Close loops, reduce noise, and set next week's direction.

## Trigger

```text
AXIS: WEEKLY REVIEW
```

## Review Questions

1. What moved forward this week?
2. What is still open?
3. What became irrelevant?
4. What admin needs clearing?
5. Who needs a reply, follow-up, or check-in?
6. What business action creates the most progress next week?
7. What personal action protects the user's life outside work?
