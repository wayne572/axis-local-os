# Access Channels

Status: optional
Purpose: Defines how the client may access this OS through approved channels.

## Default

The default access channel is Claude or the approved AI workspace.

## Optional Channels

Telegram and WhatsApp can be added later if included in the client scope.

They are access channels only.

They do not change the operating logic.

## Channel Rules

Before adding Telegram or WhatsApp:
- confirm who can access it
- confirm what data can be sent
- confirm message logging
- confirm deletion process
- confirm whether the channel is internal-only or client-facing
