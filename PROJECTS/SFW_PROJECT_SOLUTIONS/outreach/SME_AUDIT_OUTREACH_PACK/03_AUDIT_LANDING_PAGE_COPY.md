## Problem

Most SMEs do not need a big AI transformation project.

They need to fix the small operational leaks that happen every day:
- enquiries sitting too long
- follow-ups depending on memory
- staff copying data between systems
- repeated email replies
- slow quotes or proposals
- scattered documents
- manual reporting
- unclear task ownership

These problems do not always look dramatic. But over a month, they cost time, money, and momentum.

## What The Audit Does

The audit reviews 2-3 real workflows in your business and identifies where practical AI automation could help.

We look at:
- where work first enters the business
- what gets copied or repeated
- where delays happen
- which follow-ups get missed
- what tools you already use
- what would be worth improving first

## What You Get

You receive:
- a plain-English workflow review
- an automation opportunity map
- quick wins
- a recommended first build
- clear next steps

If there is nothing worth building, we will say that.

## Who It Is For

This is for UK SMEs including:
- recruitment agencies
- estate agents and property businesses
- accountancy firms
- care homes and care providers
- cleaning, facilities, trades, and local services
- small consultancies and professional services

The audit is SME-wide. The examples change by sector, but the goal is the same: reduce manual drag and improve follow-up.

## How It Works

1. Book a short call.
2. Share 2-3 workflows that feel slow, manual, or messy.
3. We review the process and tools.
4. You receive an automation map and recommendation.
5. If it makes sense, we scope a practical first build.
