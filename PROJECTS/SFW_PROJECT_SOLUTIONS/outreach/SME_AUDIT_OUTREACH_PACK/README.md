# SME Audit Outreach Pack

Status: active draft
Owner / brand: Wayne Francis
Trading entity: SF&W Project Solutions
Purpose: Start conversations with UK SMEs for the AI Automation Audit.

## Goal

Create low-pressure audit conversations with SME owners, directors, and operations leads.

The aim is not to sell AI in the first message.

The aim is to find businesses losing time to admin, follow-up, manual reporting, disconnected tools, or repeated copy-and-paste work.

## Core Offer

AI Automation Audit for SMEs

Promise:
A practical review of where the business is losing time to admin and where AI automation could improve follow-up, speed, and consistency.

## Tone

Clear.
Direct.
Useful.
Low-pressure.
No hype.

Lead with operational pain, not AI novelty.

## Primary CTA

```text
Worth a quick audit call to see where admin or follow-up could be tightened?
```

## Follow-Up Rule

No more than two follow-ups unless there is a real relationship, referral, or active conversation.

If someone says no, stop and respect it.
