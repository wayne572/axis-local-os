# Human Review Policy

Status: client-facing draft
Purpose: Define when AI outputs must be reviewed by a person.

## Core Rule

AI can assist. A responsible human decides.

## Always Review Before Use

Human review is required before using AI output for:

- client emails
- public website copy
- proposals
- pricing
- contracts
- policies
- HR matters
- personal data
- sensitive information
- regulatory or compliance topics
- financial commitments
- important business decisions

## Review Checklist

Before using an AI output, check:

- Is it accurate?
- Is it complete?
- Is it appropriate for the audience?
- Does it include anything confidential?
- Does it overpromise?
- Does it create legal, financial, HR, or compliance risk?
- Is the next action clear?

## Red Flags

Pause and review carefully if the output:

- sounds too certain
- gives legal or financial advice
- makes claims without evidence
- mentions personal data unnecessarily
- invents facts
- recommends a high-impact decision
- sounds unlike the business

## Approval Note

For important client-facing outputs, record:

- who reviewed it
- date reviewed
- decision
- changes made
