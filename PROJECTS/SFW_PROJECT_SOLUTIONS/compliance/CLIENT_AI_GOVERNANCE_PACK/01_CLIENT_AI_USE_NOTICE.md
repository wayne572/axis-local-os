# Client AI Use Notice

Status: client-facing draft
Purpose: Explain how the Client OS uses AI in plain English.

## What This System Does

Your Client OS uses AI to help organise information, draft documents, review workflows, summarise notes, support follow-up, and create repeatable operating processes.

It is designed to support human work, not replace human responsibility.

## What It May Help With

- drafting emails and documents
- summarising notes
- creating workflows and checklists
- tracking actions
- improving follow-up
- organising business context
- reviewing gaps and risks

## What It Does Not Do

The system does not make final business, legal, HR, financial, medical, or regulated decisions.

It should not be used for:

- recruitment screening or candidate ranking
- credit or lending decisions
- medical triage or diagnosis
- legal decisions
- automated decisions with legal effect
- biometric categorisation
- decisions affecting access to education or essential services

## Human Review

Important outputs should be reviewed by a responsible person before use.

This includes:

- client communications
- public statements
- legal or compliance wording
- pricing or contractual commitments
- sensitive personal data
- high-impact decisions

## Data Use

Only provide information that is necessary for the task.

Avoid adding sensitive personal information unless it is required, appropriate, and permitted.

## Usage Tracking

If usage tracking is enabled, it should be used to improve the system, understand what is helpful, and identify issues.

Usage tracking should be explained and agreed before use.

## Questions

If you are unsure whether something is appropriate to use in the Client OS, ask:

```text
Review this for data, privacy, and client risk before I use it.
```
