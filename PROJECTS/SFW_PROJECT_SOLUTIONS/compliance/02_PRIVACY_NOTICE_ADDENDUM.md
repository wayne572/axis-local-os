# Privacy Notice Addendum - Pipeline-as-a-Service
**For publication on waynefrancis.co.uk/privacy**
*v1.0 - 2026-04-30*

---

## About this Addendum

This addendum sits alongside the main Wayne Francis / SF&W Project Solutions privacy notice and explains how we handle Personal Data when delivering **Pipeline-as-a-Service** to our clients.

**Plain English summary:** When a UK business hires us to run their lead pipeline, we identify business decision-makers who match their ideal client profile, contact them on the client's behalf, qualify the responses, and book meetings. We act as a *processor* for the client. The client decides who gets contacted; we handle the mechanics.

---

## 1. Who is the Controller?

For Pipeline-as-a-Service, **our client is the Data Controller**. They decide the purpose of contacting you and what happens with your data after a meeting is booked.

**We (Wayne Francis / SF&W Project Solutions) act as the Data Processor.** We process your Personal Data only on documented instructions from our client.

If you want to know which client instructed an outreach to you, contact us using the details below and we will tell you.

## 2. What Personal Data We Process

We only process Personal Data that is:
- Business-context (work email, work phone, job title, employer, business address, LinkedIn profile)
- Publicly available or sourced from a legitimate B2B data provider
- Role-relevant to our client's offer

We do **not** process:
- Consumer / B2C contact data
- Special category data (health, race, religion, etc.)
- Children's data
- Data of any individual on our Suppression List

## 3. Where We Get the Data

- Apollo.io (B2B contact database)
- Common Room (signal enrichment)
- Public business directories and company websites
- LinkedIn public profiles
- Data the client provides directly

## 4. Lawful Basis

We rely on **legitimate interest** under Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR for B2B outreach to business decision-makers. Our client confirms its own legitimate interest assessment before we begin processing.

You have the right to object to processing at any time - see Section 7.

## 5. How Long We Keep Data

- Active prospect data: while the engagement with our client is live, plus 12 months.
- Suppression List entries: indefinitely (so we don't contact you again).
- Replies and meeting records: returned to or deleted on instruction of the client within 30 days of contract end.

## 6. Who We Share Data With

- Our client (the Controller)
- Authorised sub-processors listed in our DPA (Apollo, Anthropic, Google Workspace, Airtable, Calendly, Common Room)

Where a sub-processor is outside the UK / EEA we use Standard Contractual Clauses + UK International Data Transfer Addendum.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Object** to direct marketing - we will add you to our Suppression List and stop contact within 1 working day.
- **Access** the Personal Data we hold about you.
- **Rectification** if data is inaccurate.
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten").
- **Restriction** of processing.
- **Portability** of your data.
- **Lodge a complaint** with the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

To exercise any right, email **wayne@waynefrancis.co.uk** with the subject line "Data Request". We respond within 5 working days and complete the action within 30 days.

## 8. How We Keep Data Safe

- Encryption in transit and at rest
- MFA on all admin accounts
- Access on a need-to-know basis
- Annual GDPR + PECR training
- 24-hour breach notification to the Controller

## 9. Cookies & Marketing on Our Own Website

This Addendum covers PaaS lead processing only. Cookies and marketing on waynefrancis.co.uk are covered by the main privacy notice and our cookie banner.
