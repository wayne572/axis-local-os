## Target Clients

- SMEs with 1–20 staff
- Revenue-generating businesses (not pre-revenue)
- Owner or founder is the decision-maker
- Uses at least one digital tool
- Has at least one repeatable process eating time

### Primary Verticals
- IFA / Financial Advisory
- Recruitment
- Estate / Lettings Agents
- Commercial Cleaning
- Accountancy

### Secondary Verticals
- Mortgage Brokers
- Small Marketing Agencies
- Private Dental / Healthcare
- Small E-commerce

---

## Outcomes Delivered

- A fully operational AI operating system working from the client's business context from day one
- 16 specialist agents covering command, intake, audit, workflow design, content, sales conversations, operations, client support, pipeline tracking, deal sourcing, GDPR/compliance, and review
- Structured processes that run consistently without relying on one person's memory
- A system the client owns and controls — not a vendor dependency
- Ongoing improvement month on month via Axis OS Live

---

## What Axis AI Does Not Do

- Bespoke custom software builds from scratch
- Managed services where Wayne operates the system on the client's behalf ongoing
- Enterprise or large organisation deployments (1–20 staff only)
- One-off consultancy reports with no implementation
- Deployments for businesses with no repeatable processes or no digital tool baseline
