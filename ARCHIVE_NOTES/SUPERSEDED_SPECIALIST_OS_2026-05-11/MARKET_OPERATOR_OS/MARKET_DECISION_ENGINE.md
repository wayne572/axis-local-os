# MARKET_DECISION_ENGINE.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Define how trades are approved or rejected.

---

## 3. Core Principle

No clear setup = no trade.

---

## 4. Decision Rules

Must confirm:

- macro driver present
- correct playbook
- trend exists
- pullback present
- key level present

---

## 5. Scoring

Each rule = 1 point

4-5 -> possible trade
<4 -> reject

---

## 6. Output

YES / NO decision

---

## 7. Failure Conditions

- forcing trades
- ignoring rules
- emotional decisions

---

## 8. End State

Only high-quality trades are taken.
