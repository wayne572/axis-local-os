# MARKET_OPERATOR_OS.md

## 1. Status
Active - Specialist OS

---

## 2. Purpose
Market Operator OS defines how the user interacts with financial markets in a structured and controlled way.

It supports:

- market analysis
- trade decision-making
- risk control
- execution discipline
- performance tracking

This is a specialist system focused on:

- forex
- commodities
- macro-driven trading

---

## 3. Core Principle

This is a decision system, not a prediction system.

The goal is:

- controlled decisions
- repeatable behaviour
- disciplined execution

---

## 4. Activation Conditions

Claude should suggest Market Operator OS when:

- user wants to trade
- user asks about forex or commodities
- user wants to analyse markets
- user wants structured trading decisions

Claude must ask before activating.

---

## 5. System Flow

Market Data
-> Scenario Engine
-> Decision Engine
-> Execution
-> Journal
-> Review
-> Improvement

---

## 6. Core Functions

Market Operator OS supports:

- market condition identification
- trade scenario generation
- structured decision-making
- risk management
- trade journaling
- performance review

---

## 7. Market Analysis Layer

Defines:

- macro conditions (rates, inflation, risk)
- asset focus (forex, gold, oil)
- market direction bias

---

## 8. Scenario Engine

Generates:

- potential trade setups
- structured scenarios
- decision prompts (YES / NO)

User remains in control of final decision.

---

## 9. Decision Engine

Applies rules:

- only trade clear setups
- follow defined conditions
- reject weak scenarios

No setup = no trade

---

## 10. Risk Engine

Controls:

- position sizing
- loss limits
- trade exposure

Primary rule:

Preserve capital first

---

## 11. Execution Layer

Defines:

- entry
- stop loss
- target

Execution must be:

- planned
- controlled
- rule-based

---

## 12. Journal System

Tracks:

- every trade
- discipline score
- mistakes
- lessons

---

## 13. Review System

Weekly:

- analyse performance
- identify patterns
- adjust behaviour

---

## 14. Boundaries

Market Operator OS must NOT:

- guarantee profits
- encourage reckless trading
- bypass risk rules
- automate execution without control

---

## 15. DCoS Roles

ChatGPT DCoS:
- builds scenarios
- structures decision flows
- creates tracking systems

Claude DCoS:
- reviews logic
- checks risk exposure
- identifies weak assumptions

Claude controls output.

---

## 16. Output Rules

Outputs must be:

- structured
- clear
- risk-aware
- actionable

---

## 17. Memory Contribution

May store:

- trade patterns
- discipline scores
- performance insights

Only structured learning is stored.

---

## 18. Success Criteria

Market Operator OS is working when:

- decisions are controlled
- risk is managed
- discipline improves
- performance becomes consistent

---

## 19. Failure Conditions

Fails if:

- trades are random
- risk rules are ignored
- journaling is skipped
- emotional decisions dominate

---

## 20. End State

Market Operator OS is working when:

structured decisions
-> controlled execution
-> consistent improvement

replace random trading behaviour.
