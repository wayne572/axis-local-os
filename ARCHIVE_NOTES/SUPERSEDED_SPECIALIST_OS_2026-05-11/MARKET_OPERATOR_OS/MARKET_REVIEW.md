# MARKET_REVIEW.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Review trading performance weekly.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Improve behaviour, not just results.

---

## 4. Review Steps

Check:

- total trades
- win rate
- total P/L
- average discipline score

---

## 5. Pattern Detection

Identify:

- winning patterns
- losing patterns
- biggest mistake
- best decision

---

## 6. Adjustment Rule

Make ONE change per week.

---

## 7. Failure Conditions

- skipping review
- changing too much
- focusing only on profit

---

## 8. End State

Performance improves over time.
