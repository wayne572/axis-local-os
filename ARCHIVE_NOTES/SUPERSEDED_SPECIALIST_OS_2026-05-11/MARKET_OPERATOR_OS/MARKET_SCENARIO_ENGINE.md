# MARKET_SCENARIO_ENGINE.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Generate structured trade scenarios.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Scenarios guide decisions, not predictions.

---

## 4. Scenario Types

- valid trade
- trap trade
- no trade
- risk violation

---

## 5. Structure

Each scenario includes:

- asset
- direction
- macro reason
- chart reason
- entry idea
- stop loss
- target
- risk/reward

---

## 6. Output

Scenario + score + YES/NO decision prompt

---

## 7. Failure Conditions

- vague scenarios
- missing risk
- no structure

---

## 8. End State

User evaluates structured trade opportunities.
