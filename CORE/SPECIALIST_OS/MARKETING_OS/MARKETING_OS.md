Marketing OS defines how attention and inbound opportunities are generated.

It supports:

- visibility
- positioning
- consistent content
- inbound interest

It feeds Deal Sourcing OS.

Consistency builds attention.

Marketing is not random activity.

It is structured, repeatable output.

- user wants content ideas or structure

Claude must ask before activating.
