# MARKETING_POSITIONING.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Define how the user presents themselves clearly.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Clarity builds trust.

---

## 4. System Rules

- define audience
- define problem
- define value

---

## 5. Execution Steps

Answer:

- who do you help?
- what problem do you solve?
- how do you help?

---

## 6. Example

"I help small businesses get more clients through simple systems."

---

## 7. Output Requirement

Positioning must be:

- clear
- simple
- specific

---

## 8. Failure Conditions

- vague messaging
- trying to help everyone
- unclear value

---

## 9. End State

People understand what you do quickly.
