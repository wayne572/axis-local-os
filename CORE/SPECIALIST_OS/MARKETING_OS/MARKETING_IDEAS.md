# MARKETING_IDEAS.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Generate simple, relevant content ideas consistently.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Ideas come from real problems, not creativity pressure.

---

## 4. System Rules

- focus on audience problems
- keep ideas simple
- avoid overthinking
- generate in batches

---

## 5. Idea Sources

Use:

- common client questions
- mistakes people make
- lessons learned
- simple tips
- real experiences

---

## 6. Execution Steps

1. List 3-5 common problems
2. Turn each into a post idea
3. Keep each idea simple

---

## 7. Example

Problem:
"People struggle to get clients"

Idea:
"3 simple ways to get your first clients"

---

## 8. Output Requirement

Ideas must be:

- clear
- relevant
- easy to turn into posts

---

## 9. Failure Conditions

- overcomplicated ideas
- no clear audience link
- too generic

---

## 10. End State

You always have content ideas ready.
