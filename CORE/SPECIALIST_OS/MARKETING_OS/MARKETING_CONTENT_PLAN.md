# MARKETING_CONTENT_PLAN.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Turn ideas into a simple, repeatable content plan.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Consistency beats volume.

---

## 4. System Rules

- keep plan simple
- choose realistic frequency
- do not overcommit

---

## 5. Execution Steps

1. Choose 3-5 ideas
2. Assign days
3. define format (text, video, etc.)

---

## 6. Example

Monday -> Tip
Wednesday -> Lesson
Friday -> Insight

---

## 7. Output Requirement

Plan must be:

- simple
- repeatable
- easy to follow

---

## 8. Failure Conditions

- unrealistic schedule
- too complex
- inconsistent execution

---

## 9. End State

Content is planned and predictable.
