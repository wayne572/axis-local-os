# CLIENT_DELIVERY_COMPLETION_HANDOFF.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Define how delivery is completed and handed off.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Finish clearly and professionally.

---

## 4. System Rules

- confirm work is complete
- summarise what was delivered
- confirm client satisfaction
- define next step

---

## 5. Execution Steps

1. Review completed work
2. Summarise outcomes
3. Confirm with client
4. Close or move to next phase

---

## 6. Example

"Here's what we've completed and the outcome..."

---

## 7. Output Requirement

Client must know:

- what was delivered
- what the result is
- what happens next

---

## 8. Failure Conditions

- unclear completion
- no summary
- no next step

---

## 9. End State

Delivery ends with clarity and confidence.
