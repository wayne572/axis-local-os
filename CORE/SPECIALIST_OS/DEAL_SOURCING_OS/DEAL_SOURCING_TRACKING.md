# DEAL_SOURCING_TRACKING.md

## 1. Status
Active - Logic File (Balanced)

---

## 2. Purpose
Track all leads, interactions, and decisions in a structured and auditable way.

This ensures:

- visibility of pipeline
- compliance control
- performance tracking
- continuous improvement

---

## 3. Core Principle

If it's not tracked, it's not controlled.

Tracking must be:

- accurate
- up to date
- simple to use
- detailed enough for decisions

---

## 4. System Rules

- every lead must be logged
- every action must be recorded
- every status must be current
- no lead exists outside the tracker

---

## 5. Lead Status Categories

Each lead must have ONE active status:

- New
- Contacted
- Replied
- Qualified
- Nurture
- Not Interested
- Closed
- Compliance Hold

---

## 6. Compliance Hold Rule

Leads marked as **Compliance Hold**:

- must NOT be contacted
- must NOT progress
- must remain visible for audit
- must include reason for hold

Examples:

- AML risk
- B2C mismatch
- missing lawful basis
- sensitive data concerns

---

## 7. Required Tracking Fields

Each lead entry must include:

### Core Fields
- business name
- contact name (if available)
- contact method
- date added
- current status

---

### Activity Fields
- last action taken
- last contact date
- next step
- follow-up date

---

### Compliance Fields
- lawful basis (if applicable)
- opt-out status
- compliance flags
- compliance hold reason (if used)

---

### Outcome Fields
- response type (none / neutral / positive / opt-out)
- qualification result (yes / no / pending)
- notes (short, relevant only)

---

## 8. Execution Process

For each lead:

1. Add to tracker immediately
2. Assign initial status (New)
3. Update after outreach (Contacted)
4. Update after reply (Replied)
5. Update after qualification decision
6. Maintain next step at all times

---

## 9. Tracking Cadence

### Daily
- update all active leads
- log outreach and responses
- confirm next steps

---

### Weekly
- review pipeline
- count:
  - leads added
  - outreach sent
  - responses
  - qualified leads

---

### Monthly (Light Review)
- identify patterns
- check drop-off points
- review compliance holds

---

## 10. Retention & Data Control

- keep only useful lead data
- remove or archive stale leads
- respect opt-outs fully
- do not retain unnecessary personal data

---

## 11. Output Requirement

At any time, the tracker must show:

- current pipeline status
- next actions clearly
- compliance position
- performance indicators

---

|----------|--------|------------|-----------|----------|-------|

---

## 13. Failure Conditions

Tracking fails if:

- leads are missing
- statuses are outdated
- compliance is unclear
- next steps are missing
- notes are excessive or unclear

---

## 14. End State

Tracking is working when:

- pipeline is fully visible
- decisions are easy to make
- compliance is controlled
- performance can be improved

The tracker becomes:

A control system, not just a list.
