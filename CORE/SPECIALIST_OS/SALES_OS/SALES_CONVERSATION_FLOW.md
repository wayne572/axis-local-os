# SALES_CONVERSATION_FLOW.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Provide a simple structure for sales conversations.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Keep conversations clear and controlled.

---

## 4. Flow Structure

1. Opening
2. Discovery
3. Clarify problem
4. Confirm understanding
5. Discuss next step

---

## 5. Execution Steps

- Start relaxed
- Ask discovery questions
- Summarise what you heard
- Confirm accuracy
- Suggest next step

---

## 6. Example

"So from what you've said, your main issue is consistency with leads - is that right?"

---

## 7. Output Requirement

The conversation should end with:

- clear understanding
- agreed next step

---

## 8. Failure Conditions

- unclear direction
- jumping between topics
- no next step

---

## 9. End State

Conversations feel structured and productive.
