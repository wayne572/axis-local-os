# SALES_HANDOFF_TO_DELIVERY.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Define how to move from conversation to delivery.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Clarity before action.

---

## 4. System Rules

Before handoff:

- confirm the problem
- confirm expectations
- confirm next step

---

## 5. Execution Steps

- summarise conversation
- confirm agreement
- define next action
- transition to delivery

---

## 6. Example

"Based on what we discussed, the next step would be [clear action]. Does that work for you?"

---

## 7. Output Requirement

The handoff must include:

- clear summary
- agreed direction
- defined next step

---

## 8. Failure Conditions

- unclear agreement
- missing expectations
- rushed transition

---

## 9. End State

The prospect becomes a client with clarity and confidence.
