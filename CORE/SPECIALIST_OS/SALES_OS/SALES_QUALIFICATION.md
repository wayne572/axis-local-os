# SALES_QUALIFICATION.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Define how to decide if a prospect is worth pursuing.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Not every conversation should become a deal.

---

## 4. System Rules

Check for:

- clear problem
- real need
- ability to act
- communication quality

---

## 5. Execution Steps

After discovery, ask yourself:

- Can I actually help?
- Do they care about solving this?
- Are they ready to act?

---

## 6. Output Format

Mark each lead:

- YES -> continue
- MAYBE -> follow-up later
- NO -> stop

---

## 7. Failure Conditions

- chasing poor-fit leads
- ignoring red flags
- continuing without clarity

---

## 8. End State

You focus only on strong opportunities.
