# SALES_FOLLOW_UP.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Define how to follow up without pressure.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Follow-up is helpful, not pushy.

---

## 4. System Rules

- Keep it short
- Add value where possible
- Do not chase aggressively

---

## 5. Execution Steps

Send:

- follow-up after conversation
- reminder if no response
- final message

---

## 6. Example Messages

Follow-up:

"Just checking in - happy to continue the conversation when it suits you."

Final:

"No pressure at all - feel free to reach out if it becomes relevant."

---

## 7. Failure Conditions

- sending too many messages
- sounding desperate
- repeating the same message

---

## 8. End State

Follow-ups feel natural and respectful.
