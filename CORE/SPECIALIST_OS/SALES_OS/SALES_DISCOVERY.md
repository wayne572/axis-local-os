# SALES_DISCOVERY.md

## 1. Status
Active - Execution Sub-File

---

## 2. Purpose
Define how to run structured discovery conversations.

This ensures:

- you understand the prospect properly
- you avoid guessing
- conversations feel natural and useful

---

## 3. Core Principle

Do not sell before you understand.

Discovery comes first.

---

## 4. System Rules

- Ask, do not assume
- Let the prospect speak more than you
- Focus on their situation, not your solution
- Do not rush to "pitch"

---

## 5. Execution Steps

Ask simple, natural questions:

1. What are you currently trying to improve?
2. What's the biggest challenge right now?
3. What have you tried so far?
4. What would a good result look like?
5. Why is this important now?

---

## 6. Example

"You mentioned you're struggling with lead flow - what's currently not working?"

---

## 7. Output Requirement

By the end of discovery, you should know:

- the real problem
- the importance of solving it
- whether you can help

---

## 8. Failure Conditions

Discovery fails if:

- you talk more than the prospect
- you pitch too early
- you skip key questions

---

## 9. End State

Discovery is working when:

- the problem is clear
- the conversation feels natural
- next steps are obvious
