## Core Purpose

- record what was decided
- record what was built or completed
- record what still needs to happen
- protect continuity between sessions

---

## Rules

- do not summarise conversation — summarise outcomes
- do not include anything that was discussed but not decided
- keep every section brief and factual
- use plain language — no jargon
- if nothing was completed in a section, write: None this session
