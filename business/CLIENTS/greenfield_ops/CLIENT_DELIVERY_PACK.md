# Greenfield Ops — Delivery Pack

Items the client will receive at the end of delivery. Each is sourced from onboarding outputs and the workflow set.

## Pack contents

1. **Onboarding artefact** — `INTAKE.md` + `ONBOARDING.md` (already in client folder)
2. **Workflow set** — `CLIENT_WORKFLOWS.md` (3 workflows: Client Onboarding, Delivery Tracking, Handover Preparation)
3. **Delivery checklist** — `DELIVERY_CHECKLIST.md` populated from sources
4. **Handover record** — `HANDOVER.md` (created at handover stage)
5. **Compliance reference** — links to `business/HANDOVER/COMPLIANCE_PROTOCOL.md` and `GDPR_PROTOCOL.md` for the client's awareness
6. **Post-lock support guide** — pointer to `business/HANDOVER/POST_LOCK_SUPPORT.md`

## Out of scope

- Pricing or commercial terms (Pricing Neutrality)
- Marketing assets (Marketing OS not activated)
- Deal sourcing infrastructure (Deal Sourcing OS not activated)
- Custom code / bespoke build (per Axis "What we don't do")

## Quality bar

The client should be able to use everything in this pack on Day 8 without further explanation.
