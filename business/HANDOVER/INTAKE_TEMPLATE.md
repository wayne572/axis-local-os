# Client Intake

> **For the client to complete.** Issued by Wayne / the `handover` agent before onboarding begins. Read by the `onboarding` agent at Block A. Captures business context only — pricing is reviewed in a separate phase, so do not solicit budget or pricing here.

Take your time. Shorter, honest answers are better than long polished ones.

---

## Section 1 — Business Identity

- Business name:
- Owner:
- Authorised signatory (the person who can approve the deployment):
- Sector / industry:
- Size (team headcount; revenue band if known):
- Registered details (Companies House number / charity number / sole trader, if applicable):

---

## Section 2 — Current Setup

- What tools or systems are currently in use? (CRM, email, spreadsheets, project tools, anything you actively use)
- How is work currently managed day-to-day?
- Are any automations already in place? (Zapier, Make, in-app rules, anything else)

---

## Section 3 — Problems & Friction

- What feels messy, slow, or unclear right now?
- Where are the biggest bottlenecks?
- What's currently not working the way you'd want it to?

---

## Section 4 — Lead Flow & Growth

- How do leads currently come in? (referrals, website, ads, networking, inbound, outbound)
- What channels are you actively using?
- Where do you feel opportunities are being missed?

---

## Section 5 — Operations & Admin

- What takes the most manual time each week?
- Where is repetition highest? (the same task, done over and over)
- What would you remove from your week if you could?

---

## Section 6 — Priorities

- What needs fixing first?
- What would make the biggest impact in your day-to-day?
- 30–60 day focus — what do you most want to be true 60 days from now?

---

## Section 7 — Notes

Anything else we should know? Free-text — context, history, constraints, preferences.

---
