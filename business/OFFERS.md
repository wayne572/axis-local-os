## 1. Solo Operator OS

### Audience

- solo founders
- freelancers
- consultants
- coaches
- creators
- carers or non-technical operators
- side-business owners

### Problem

The person is running too much from memory, email, WhatsApp, notes, scattered documents, or reactive decisions.

### Outcome

A personal operating system that helps organise daily priorities, weekly review, follow-ups, ideas, decisions, relationships, admin, and solo business actions.

### Current Pricing

- Starter: GBP 1,495
- Standard: GBP 2,495
- Premium: GBP 3,995

Recommended default: Standard.

## 2. SME AI Automation Audit

### Audience

SMEs that know AI or better systems could help, but do not know where to start.

### Problem

The business is losing time, margin, or control through manual work, scattered follow-up, repeated admin, unclear handovers, or inconsistent client communication.

### Outcome

A practical audit report showing current workflows, pain points, AI opportunity map, priority recommendations, risk/governance notes, and recommended build scope.

### Current Pricing

- Lite: GBP 2,500
- Core: GBP 4,500
- Deep Dive: GBP 6,500

Recommended default: Core.

## 3. Client OS Build / Client OS Growth

### Audience

SMEs ready to build and adopt a working operating system around clients, sales, delivery, follow-ups, decisions, and team rhythm.

### Problem

Client information is scattered. Leads and follow-ups are hard to track. Delivery relies on memory. Handover is inconsistent. Decisions are not always recorded.

### Outcome

A client-owned operating system with command centre, client memory, pipeline/follow-up workflow, delivery tracker, decision log, templates, governance notes, handover, first 7 days adoption, and 30-day review.

### Current Pricing

- Foundation: GBP 6,500
- Growth: GBP 9,500
- Enterprise: GBP 12,500

Recommended default: Growth.

### Selling Rule

Do not sell Growth as "we build you an AI OS" only.

```text
Audit, scope, build, test, train, and review a practical AI-supported operating system around the agreed workflows your team actually uses.
```

### Outcome

### Current Pricing

- Growth: GBP 995/month

## What We Do Not Sell

- vague AI transformation
- unscoped automation
- cheap template-only work positioned as a full implementation
- guaranteed revenue outcomes
- guaranteed time savings without measurement
- regulated decision automation without explicit review
- recruitment screening or candidate ranking
- medical, credit, legal-effect, biometric, safeguarding, or education-access decision systems
- client systems that bypass consent, data protection, or compliance boundaries
